    • introduction - what is a neural network?

    • Modern computational neural networks are loosely based off of biological networks.
    • essentially, input information is vectorized and passed through a number of internal layers. these internal layers generaize the more granular inital input into broader concepts - for instance, converting a group of pixels into a circle or edge.
