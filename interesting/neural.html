introduction - what is a neural network?

  • Modern computational networks are loosely based off of biological networks. Essentially, input is vectorized and passed through a number of internal layers. These internal layers generaize the more granular inital input into broader concepts - for instance, converting a group of pixels into an edge and then a circle.
